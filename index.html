About This Project

Site Created By: Matthew Turner

Reason: This site exists simply for experimentation purposes. I myself have always found parallax scrolling websites beautiful and awesome. Turns out the math is really simple. Anyway upon learning how to do parallax scrolling I decided it would be pretty neat to make a small website that expands upon the information given in my final project for chemistry. It's useful for anyone who may want more information on how LCDs work and I figure that the knowledge I gain from creating this won't go to waste. Also if Mrs. Mauer would like to give me some extra credit I wouldn't be opposed to the idea. ;)

Liquid Crystal Displays

While I'm fairly certain that all of you probably know what an LCD is by now (if not click here), I think its fair to say that the majority of you probably don't know just how many different types there are. It's also possible that you hear some of the specifications and numbers people talk about but have no idea what they mean. This section will hopefully clear some of that up for you.
